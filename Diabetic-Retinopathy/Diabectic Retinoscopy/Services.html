Retina Scan

Our clinic utilizes advanced Machine Learning (ML) algorithms to analyze retinal images and detect the early signs of diabetic retinopathy. Early diagnosis is crucial for timely intervention and preventing vision loss.

The retina scan is a painless and non-invasive procedure that allows our ophthalmologists to assess the health of your retina and identify any abnormalities associated with diabetic retinopathy. By detecting changes in the retina at an early stage, we can provide prompt treatment to protect your eyesight and prevent further complications.

Book an Appointment

Schedule a consultation with our team of ophthalmologists to receive personalized treatment plans tailored to your unique needs and the progression of diabetic retinopathy. We prioritize your vision health and overall well-being.

To book an appointment, please use our online appointment booking system or contact our clinic directly. Our friendly staff will assist you in selecting a convenient date and time for your visit.

Diabetes Management Education

As part of our holistic approach, we offer educational resources and guidance to help you manage diabetes effectively. Proper diabetes management is crucial in reducing the risk and progression of diabetic retinopathy.

Our team of experts will provide you with valuable information on diabetes management techniques, lifestyle modifications, and best practices to maintain optimal blood sugar levels. By taking control of your diabetes, you can protect your vision and overall health.
