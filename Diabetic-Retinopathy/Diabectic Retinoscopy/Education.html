Diabetic Management Education

Managing diabetes effectively is crucial for maintaining your health. Follow these steps to manage your diabetes:

  1. Monitor your blood sugar levels regularly.
  2. Follow a balanced and healthy diet. Limit sugar and processed foods.
  3. Stay physically active with regular exercise.
  4. Take prescribed medications as directed by your healthcare provider.
  5. Maintain a healthy weight to help control blood sugar levels.
  6. Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
  7. Manage stress through relaxation techniques and hobbies.
  8. Get regular eye exams to detect any diabetic retinopathy.
  9. Quit smoking if you are a smoker.
  10. Communicate with your healthcare team and attend regular check-ups.
